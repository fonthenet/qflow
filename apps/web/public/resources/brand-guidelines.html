Do's & Don'ts

✔ Do

  • Use the blue gradient for primary backgrounds
  • Keep text concise and action-oriented
  • Use Noto Sans Arabic for all Arabic text
  • Show the WhatsApp flow as the hero use case
  • Use sector-specific language (clinic, bank, etc.)
  • Include RTL layout for Arabic materials

✘ Don't

  • Use the logo smaller than 24px
  • Place the logo on busy photo backgrounds
  • Mix French and Arabic on the same page
  • Use technical terms (API, webhook, etc.) in customer-facing materials
  • Change brand colors or use gradients not in the palette
  • Use stock photos of Western office settings
