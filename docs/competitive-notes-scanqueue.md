## Public Product Pattern

ScanQueue presents a very clear product story:

- scan QR code
- join queue from phone
- wait anywhere
- receive notification
- staff manage queue from dashboard

This is strong because the customer story is immediate and the setup promise is low-friction.

## Product Ideas Worth Borrowing

- immediate setup promise
- QR-first intake
- no-app visitor flow
- real-time business dashboard
- industry-specific examples

## Product Ideas To Improve

QueueFlow should do these better:

- expand beyond queues into bookings, reservations, check-ins, and service handoff
- make dashboard architecture truly category-aware
- provide stronger platform ownership and super-admin control
- use smarter onboarding to enable the right modules automatically

## Clear Positioning Difference

### ScanQueue

"Join a queue by QR code and get notified when it is your turn."

### QueueFlow

"Run customer flow for service businesses: arrivals, waiting, bookings, reservations, and service handoff in one system."
