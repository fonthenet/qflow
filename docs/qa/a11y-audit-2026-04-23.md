## Closed on 2026-04-23 follow-up

### SERIOUS

- **S-01 — SC 3.1.1** Web kiosk: `html[lang]` and `html[dir]` not updated on client-side locale switch.
  Fixed: added `useEffect` in `kiosk-view.tsx` that calls `document.documentElement.lang = locale` and `document.documentElement.dir = locale === 'ar' ? 'rtl' : 'ltr'` whenever `locale` or `dir` changes.

- **S-02 — SC 1.3.1** Electron kiosk: step-progress dots were unlabeled `<div>` elements.
  Fixed: `renderSteps()` in `kiosk.js` now wraps the container in `role="list" aria-label="Progress"`, each dot becomes `role="listitem" aria-label="Step N of M"` with a visually-hidden `<span class="sr-only">` text. Added `Progress` and `Step {n} of {total}` translations to the FR/AR dictionaries. Added `.sr-only` utility class to `kiosk.css`.

- **S-03 — SC 1.3.1** Web kiosk: intake inputs had no programmatic label association.
  Fixed: all four branches of `renderKioskIntakeField` (wilaya select, wilaya text fallback, age, generic) now use `htmlFor={kiosk-field-${field.key}}` on `<label>` and matching `id` on the control. The hardcoded name/phone fallback in `renderKioskIntakeFields` uses `id="kiosk-field-name"` and `id="kiosk-field-phone"`.

- **S-04 — SC 4.1.2** Expo: `<ActivityIndicator>` had no `accessibilityLabel`.
  Fixed: loading-screen `ActivityIndicator` and button-spinner `ActivityIndicator` in `[slug].tsx` both now carry `accessibilityLabel={t('kiosk.loadingKiosk')}`.

- **S-05 — SC 2.4.1** Web kiosk: no skip-navigation link.
  Fixed: skip-nav `<a href="#kiosk-main-content">` added at the top of the `KioskView` return. The body div receives `id="kiosk-main-content"`. Link uses Tailwind `sr-only focus:not-sr-only` pattern. Not added to Electron or Expo (touch hardware; no benefit). FR/AR translations for `Skip to main content` added to `messages.ts`.

### MODERATE

- **M-01 — SC 1.4.11** Electron kiosk: `outline: none` + `box-shadow` fails Windows High Contrast mode (forced-colors).
  Fixed: `kiosk.css` now includes `@media (forced-colors: active)` block that restores `outline: 2px solid ButtonText` on `:focus` for all interactive classes and suppresses box-shadow overrides.

- **M-02 — SC 1.4.3** Web: `text-slate-400` (#94a3b8 ≈ 3.1:1 on white) used for small body text.
  Fixed: the following classes in `kiosk-view.tsx` changed to `text-slate-500` (#64748b, 4.5:1): "Fill in your details to get started" subtitle, Departments chip label, "Served ahead of standard visits" priority subtitle, "Estimated Wait Time" label, "Get Notified" label, ticket-type tracking label, and "Closed" spans in the hours table. Purely decorative metadata (timestamp, optional label) left at `text-slate-400` as they are not conveying information.

- **M-03 — SC 1.3.1** Expo: decorative `Ionicons` inside labelled `TouchableOpacity` cards caused duplicate narration.
  Fixed: department card icon, service card icon, priority flag icon, and all card chevron icons in `[slug].tsx` now carry `importantForAccessibility="no"` — the parent `TouchableOpacity` already has `accessibilityLabel` so the inner icons are suppressed from the accessibility tree.

- **M-04 — SC 1.1.1** QR images without adjacent visible text for blind users.
  Fixed: in `kiosk-view.tsx`, a `<div>` showing the full tracking URL (`/q/{qr_token}`) is rendered beneath the QR image when a ticket `qr_token` is available. Text is 9px, break-all, capped to the QR width. Blind users who cannot see the QR can read or be read the URL by a sighted companion or dictate it manually.

- **M-05 — SC 1.3.1** Web: hours `<table>` lacked `<caption>` and `<thead>`.
  Fixed: `kiosk-view.tsx` hours table now has `<caption className="sr-only">{t('Business Hours')}</caption>`, `<thead>` with sr-only `<th scope="col">` for Day and Hours columns, and data rows wrapped in `<tbody>`. FR/AR translations for `Day` and `Hours` added to `messages.ts`.
